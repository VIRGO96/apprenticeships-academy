Information For Employers

With wages starting at £3.30 per hour and a chance to claim up to £1,500 for each apprentice you employ, the business case starts here.

Apprenticeships are a great way to ensure your workforce has the practical skills and qualifications to meet the evolving needs of your business.

Do you have a business that is interested in hiring an apprentice? Read the frequently asked questions below to learn more.

What is an Apprenticeship?

An Apprenticeship is a work-based training programme designed around the needs of employers, which leads to nationally-recognised qualifications for the apprentice.

Our programmes will ensure that your apprentice is properly trained to perform the job you need them to do and you may be eligible to receive government funding.

You can use Apprenticeships to recruit and train new employees as well as develop your existing staff to take your business where it needs to go.

Who can apply?

Any suitable company can apply.

Why the ASA?

We provide a free advice and recruitment service tailored to your needs. Every employer will be aided by one of our experienced Apprenticeship Engagers who will take you through every step of the process and ensure your business gets the best from the programme.

Every Apprentice will be allocated a dedicated Trainer Assessor to offer advice and guidance and plan a bespoke Apprenticeship programme. Trainer Assessors support Apprentices throughout the programme in liaison with you as the Employer.

When does it begin?

Recruitment is all year round.

Other reasons to apply?

By employing an Apprentice you will be employing an enthusiastic, motivated, flexible and loyal member of staff that can fill the skills gaps in your workforce with fresh, young talent.

You will receive dedicated advice and support from your personal ASA Apprenticeship Engager who can also help you plan your organisation’s ongoing training and development.

We can also help you secure government grants awarded to businesses that employ Apprentices.

For advice and guidance contact us by email or phone on apprenticeships@redbridge-college.ac.uk or 020 8548 7400.
