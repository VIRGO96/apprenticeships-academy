WELCOME TO THE APPRENTICESHIP SERVICES ACADEMY

ASA is a business unit of Redbridge College, with offices in London, Kent and Hertfordshire. We work with businesses and individuals to develop opportunities to take part in the National Apprenticeship Scheme. Whether you are a business looking to employ an apprentice or a young person looking to do an apprenticeship, we will help you decide what, where, how and when is best for you with our free, no pressure service..

Case Studies

Student Success

Clelia Allen joined Redbridge College on a Level 2 Business Administration Apprenticeship and really enjoyed her learning experience as it was a new teaching style that she found truly engaging.

During her time on the Apprenticeship programme Clelia was an excellent student ambassador and upon completion of her Apprenticeship she managed to obtain a fulltime position as receptionist at the Chadwell Heath Campus.

Clelia went on to take secondment to Learner Financial Support Services, really excelled in her capabilities and was then able to apply for a current Admin position in the Hairdressing curriculum area which she has been doing since 2013.

Andrew Taiwo is the manager of Concordia Health Limited, where he hired apprentice Somaya Sheikh.

He says, “As the manager of the practice and working with Somaya Sheikh, I found her to be an enthusiastic individual who is willing to learn, very punctual and it is very encouraging to see a young lady like her willing to put effort into her future.”

Sara Simpson is employed as a Level 2 Apprentice at a day nursery in Hertfordshire. She says “The Apprenticeship Team helped to support me whenever I needed it. I enjoyed all of the activities and the way they teach us. I am able to learn new things and this has helped me in the workplace.”

Rachel Arnold is also employed as a Level 2 Apprentice at a nursery in Hertfordshire. “This course has given me a broad aspect of childcare. The teaching has been excellent and the skills help me in the workplace.“
